After you submit提交后：

Within 24 hours — Our team reviews your request and confirms which samples are most relevant to your application.24 小时内 — 我们的团队将审核您的申请，并确认哪些样品最符合您的应用需求。

Within 3–5 business days — Samples are prepared and dispatched via international courier. We will send you a tracking number.3-5 个工作日内 — 样品将准备完毕并通过国际快递寄出。我们会向您发送追踪号码。

Sample shipments are provided at no charge for qualified buyers. Courier costs may apply depending on destination — we will confirm this before dispatch.符合条件的买家可免费获得样品。快递费用可能因目的地而异 — 我们将在发货前与您确认。
