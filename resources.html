FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?最小起订量（MOQ）是多少？

Our standard MOQ varies by product. RTD tea bases start at 100L, tea extracts and powders start at 10kg, and OEM/ODM programs typically start from 500L depending on formulation complexity.我们的 MOQ 根据产品类型不同而不同：即饮茶基底通常从 100L 起订，茶萃取液与粉末通常从 10kg 起订，OEM/ODM 项目通常从 500L 起订，具体取决于配方复杂度。

What are your typical lead times?常规交期是多久？

Stock products usually take 2-3 weeks, while custom formulations take 4-6 weeks. For new OEM launches, we recommend locking requirements 6-8 weeks in advance to secure production slots.现货产品通常 2-3 周交付，定制配方通常 4-6 周。对于新 OEM 上市项目，建议至少提前 6-8 周锁定需求，以便排产和验证。

Can I request samples before placing a bulk order?下大货前可以先申请样品吗？

Yes. We provide sample packs for RTD bases and concentrates, typically shipped within 3-5 business days. Sample fees can be credited against qualified bulk orders.可以。我们提供 RTD 基底和浓缩液样品，通常在 3-5 个工作日内寄出。符合条件的大货订单可抵扣样品费用。

What certifications do your products have?你们有哪些认证？

We support projects with ISO 22000/FSSC 22000 quality systems and related compliance documentation. Certificates and supporting files can be shared during technical review.我们可配合提供 ISO 22000/FSSC 22000 质量体系相关资料及合规文件。证书与配套文件可在技术评审阶段提供。

How do you handle shipping and logistics?物流和运输如何安排？

We support FOB, CIF, and DDP terms with qualified logistics partners. Air and sea options are available, and shipping plans are matched to product sensitivity and timeline requirements.我们支持 FOB、CIF、DDP 等贸易条款，并与稳定物流伙伴协作。可提供空运与海运方案，并根据产品敏感性和时效需求制定运输计划。

Do you offer custom formulation services?是否提供定制配方服务？

Yes. Our R&D team supports flavor direction, prototype development, and stability testing for beverage and food applications, then transfers the approved formula to pilot and production stages.提供。我们的研发团队支持风味方向确认、样品开发与稳定性测试，并将确认配方推进到小试和量产阶段。

What is your quality control process?你们的质量控制流程是什么？

Each batch follows incoming inspection, in-process control, and final release testing. Batch traceability, COA records, and retention samples are maintained for consistent quality management.每个批次都执行来料检验、过程控制和成品放行检测。我们保留批次追溯、COA 文件和留样记录，用于持续质量管理。

Can I visit your production facility?可以参观工厂吗？

Factory visits are welcome by appointment. We can arrange production, blending, and QA process walkthroughs. Please book at least two weeks in advance.欢迎预约工厂参观。可安排生产、拼配与质量流程讲解。建议至少提前两周预约，以便协调接待与产线安排。
