FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ varies by product. For RTD tea bases, the minimum is 100L. For tea extracts and powders, the minimum is 10kg. For OEM/ODM services, we offer flexible MOQs starting from 500L depending on formulation complexity. Please contact our sales team for customized quotations.

What are your typical lead times?

Standard lead times are 2-3 weeks for stock products and 4-6 weeks for custom formulations. Rush orders may be available at a premium. All timelines depend on order size, product complexity, and current production schedule. We recommend placing orders 6-8 weeks before your target launch date for OEM/ODM projects.

Can I request samples before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely. We offer sample packs (typically 500ml-1L) of our RTD bases and concentrate formulations. Sample requests are usually fulfilled within 3-5 business days. A nominal sample fee may apply, which is waived for orders exceeding 500L. Contact our sample request form to get started.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified by leading international standards including ISO 22000, FDA compliance for food safety, and FSSC 22000. We maintain full traceability and documentation for all raw materials. Certificate copies are available upon request. For detailed certification information, visit our Quality section or contact our compliance team.

How do you handle shipping and logistics?

We arrange shipping through trusted logistics partners with temperature control for sensitive products. We offer FOB, CIF, and DDP terms. Typical shipping methods include air freight (3-5 days) and ocean freight (15-20 days). All shipments include proper labeling, documentation, and insurance. We also provide real-time tracking for all orders.

Do you offer custom formulation services?

Yes, our R&D team specializes in custom tea formulations for beverages, functional drinks, and food applications. We conduct market research, flavor profiling, and stability testing. Standard development timelines are 4-8 weeks. An upfront R&D fee applies, typically offset by your first production order.

What's your quality control process?

Every batch undergoes multi-stage QC: incoming material inspection, in-process monitoring, final product testing (microbiology, chemistry, sensory), and stability testing. We maintain detailed batch records and can provide full traceability. Our QA team follows strict protocols aligned with ISO 22000 and FDA standards.

Can I visit your production facility?

Factory tours are welcome by appointment. We offer comprehensive facility tours showcasing our processing, blending, and quality control labs. Tours typically take 2-3 hours. Please schedule at least 2 weeks in advance through our inquiry form to arrange transportation and ensure production continuity.
