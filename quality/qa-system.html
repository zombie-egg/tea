Our quality assurance system runs from the moment raw materials arrive at our factory to the moment finished products leave for your market. Every critical point is monitored, documented, and traceable.我们的质量保证体系从原材料抵达工厂的那一刻起，一直持续到成品运往您的市场。每一个关键点都受到监控、记录和追溯。

Our QA Philosophy品控理念

Most quality problems in beverage manufacturing happen before production begins — in the raw material, in the sourcing decision, in the supplier relationship. We control this risk by sourcing the majority of our tea ingredients from our own Yuanjin factories in Chaozhou and Guangzhou. When you buy our products, the supply chain does not start at a trading company — it starts at the tea garden. ——Yuanjin）工厂。因此，当您选购我们的产品时，其供应链并非始于贸易公司，而是直接溯源至最初的茶园。

QA at Every Stage全链路品控

Stage 1 — Raw Material Intake阶段一 — 原料入库检验

Every incoming batch of tea material is inspected against specification before entering production. Checks include: appearance, aroma, moisture content, and — for extract production — tea solids and colour value. Non-conforming material is rejected at this stage, not after processing.每一批入库的茶叶原料在进入生产环节前，均需依据既定规范进行检验。检验项目包括：外观、香气、水分含量；若用于提取物生产，还需检测茶叶固形物含量及色度值。不符合标准的原料将在本阶段即被拒收，绝不流入后续加工环节

Stage 2 — Production Process Control阶段二 — 生产过程控制

Critical control points are monitored continuously throughout production. For extract lines, this includes extraction temperature, time, and concentration. For tea ingredient processing, this includes roasting temperature, drying parameters, and finishing grade. All CCP data is logged and retained. CCPCCP）数据均会被详细记录并归档保存。

Stage 3 — In-Process Sampling阶段三 — 过程抽样检验

Samples are taken at defined intervals during each production run and tested against the approved specification. Deviations trigger an immediate production hold and root cause investigation before the run continues.在每一轮生产运行期间，我们会按预设的时间间隔进行抽样，并依据经批准的质量规范对样本进行检测。一旦发现任何偏差，生产工作将立即暂停，并启动根本原因调查，直至问题解决后方恢复生产。

Stage 4 — Finished Product QC阶段四 — 成品质量检验

Every batch of finished product is tested before release. For extracts and concentrates: Brix, colour index, pH, and microbial testing. For tea ingredients: moisture, aroma assessment, appearance grading, and foreign material check. Products that do not meet specification are not released — they are reprocessed or destroyed.Brix）、色度指数、pH值及微生物检测；针对茶叶原料，检测项目则包括：水分含量、香气评估、外观分级及异物检查。任何不符合质量规范的产品均不予放行，将视情况进行返工处理或予以销毁。

Stage 5 — Traceability & Documentation阶段五 — 追溯与文档管理

Every production batch is assigned a unique lot number that traces back to the raw material intake records. If a quality issue is identified post-shipment, we can identify the exact batch, the production date, the raw material source, and the QC records within 24 hours.每一批次的产品均被赋予一个唯一的批次编号，该编号可追溯至相应的原料入库记录。若在产品发货后发现任何质量问题，我们可在24小时内迅速锁定涉事的确切批次、生产日期、原料来源以及相关的质量检验记录。
