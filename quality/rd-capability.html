Fresh-Extraction Technology鲜萃技术

Our proprietary fresh-extraction process is the technical foundation of our RTD and extract product lines. It preserves the natural volatile aroma compounds and active tea constituents at the point of extraction — delivering a flavour profile that standard brewing or concentrate methods cannot replicate.RTD）及茶提取物产品线的坚实技术基石。该工艺能在提取的瞬间，最大限度地保留茶叶中天然的挥发性芳香化合物及活性成分——从而呈现出一种标准冲泡法或浓缩还原法所无法复刻的独特风味轮廓。

Aroma-Locking Technology锁香技术

A specialist capability developed within our Srimark R&D centre. We use professional aroma-locking processes to fix the aromatic profile of tea ingredients and finished products — ensuring that the character of the tea survives processing, packaging, storage, and transit.这是一项由我们Srimark研发中心自主开发的专业核心能力。我们运用专业的锁香工艺，将茶叶原料及成品中的芳香特征牢牢锁定——确保茶叶的本真风味在历经加工、包装、仓储及运输等全流程后，依然能完好如初地得以保留。

Custom Blending定制拼配

We blend across all six major tea categories — combining varieties, roast levels, and aromatic profiles to create proprietary tea blends for our clients. Custom blends are developed to a target flavour brief and locked once approved — they are not shared with other clients.我们精通中国六大茶类的拼配技艺——通过巧妙组合不同的茶叶品种、烘焙火候及香气特征，为客户量身打造独一无二的专属拼配茶。每一款定制拼配茶均依据客户预设的风味简报进行研发；一旦配方获得批准，即被予以锁定——绝不会向其他客户公开或共享。

Scenario-Based Product Development场景化产品研发

We develop products with their end-use environment in mind. A table beverage for a Chinese hot pot restaurant has different requirements from a premium RTD for European retail. Our R&D team designs for the scenario — flavour intensity, serve format, cost structure, and shelf-life requirements — not just the formula in isolation.我们在研发产品时，始终将产品的最终应用场景置于首位。例如，一款专供中式火锅店的佐餐饮料，其研发需求与一款面向欧洲零售市场的精品即饮茶截然不同。我们的研发团队在设计方案时，绝非仅局限于孤立的配方本身，而是会全方位考量应用场景的各项要素——包括风味强度、饮用形式、成本结构以及保质期要求等。
