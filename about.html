How We Built It

Real company development timeline

Click a year to view the Yuanjin Group and TeaSourcex development story.

2007

Foundation

Yuanjin Group established in Guangzhou.

Operations began with tea ingredient sourcing and processing for the domestic Chinese beverage market.

2010s

Factory Expansion

Yuanjin Guangzhou factory developed.

The factory specialized in imported black tea processing and global tea ingredient supply, establishing the foundation for international export capability.

2020

Chaozhou Factory

Yuanjin Chaozhou factory commissioned.

Located near Fenghuang Mountain within the Phoenix Dancong GI protection zone, the facility became a leading Phoenix Dancong production base in Chaozhou.

2021

Srimark R&D Centre

Beverage R&D centre established.

Dedicated to formulation innovation, aroma-locking technology and modern tea beverage applications for international markets.

2022

Xiaocha Fresh Technology

Fresh-extraction RTD tea technology launched.

The brand applied 20 years of R&D expertise to zero-additive fresh-extraction RTD tea bases and concentrates, including a 3-second serve standard for F&B channels.

2024

TeaSourcex

International export brand established.

TeaSourcex brings the group’s product range, manufacturing capability and R&D expertise to beverage brands, manufacturers and F&B operators worldwide.
