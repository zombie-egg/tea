Company Development公司发展

Company development timeline公司发展时间轴

2007

Foundation2007年 — 奠基

Yuanjin Group established in Guangzhou. Operations begin with tea ingredient sourcing and processing for the domestic Chinese beverage market.远津集团（Yuanjin Group）于广州成立。初期业务聚焦于为中国国内饮料市场提供茶叶原料的采购与加工服务。

2010s

Factory Expansion2010年代 — 工厂扩建

Yuanjin (Guangzhou) factory developed, specialising in imported black tea processing and global tea ingredient supply. The foundation of our international export capability is established.远津（广州）工厂建成投产，专注于进口红茶的加工以及全球茶叶原料的供应。至此，集团的国际出口能力基础正式确立。

2020

Chaozhou Factory2020年 — 潮州工厂

Yuanjin (Chaozhou) factory commissioned — adjacent to Fenghuang Mountain, within the Phoenix Dancong GI protection zone. Becomes the leading Phoenix Dancong production facility in the Chaozhou region. Annual capacity: 8,000 tonnes. 12 high-speed automated production lines. Total facility area: 30,000m².远津（潮州）工厂正式启用——该厂毗邻凤凰山，坐落于凤凰单丛茶地理标志（GI）保护区内。现已成为潮州地区首屈一指的凤凰单丛茶生产基地。年产能达8,000吨，配备12条高速自动化生产线，厂区总占地面积达30,000平方米。

2021

Srimark R&D Centre2021年 — Srimark研发中心

The Srimark (SRI/MAR) beverage R&D centre is established — dedicated to formulation innovation, aroma-locking technology, and the development of modern tea beverage applications for international markets.Srimark（SRI/MAR）饮料研发中心正式成立——致力于配方创新、锁香技术研发，以及面向国际市场的现代化茶饮料应用开发。

2022

Xiaocha Fresh Technology2022年 — Xiaocha Fresh Technology

Xiaocha Fresh Beverage Technology launched — applying 20 years of R&D expertise to a new product category: zero-additive, fresh-extraction RTD tea bases and concentrates. The 3-second serve standard is developed for the F&B channel.“Xiaocha Fresh Beverage Technology”（小茶鲜饮科技）品牌正式发布——将集团20年的研发专长应用于全新的产品品类：零添加、鲜萃工艺的即饮（RTD）茶基底及浓缩液。同时，针对餐饮渠道（F&B Channel）推出了“3秒出杯”的快速制作标准。

2024

TeaSourcex2024年 — TeaSourcex

TeaSourcex is established as the dedicated international export brand of Yuanjin Group — bringing the full product range, manufacturing capability, and R&D expertise of the group directly to beverage brands, manufacturers, and F&B operators worldwide.TeaSourcex 作为远津集团专设的国际出口品牌正式成立——旨在将集团全系产品、制造实力及研发专长直接输送给全球各地的饮料品牌商、制造商及餐饮运营企业。
