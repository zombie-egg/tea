Freshly Pressed Fruit Tea Series鲜萃果茶系列

01

Ya-Shi Aromatic Lemon Tea鸭屎香柠檬茶

Category类别
Freshly pressed fruit tea鲜萃果茶
Method方式
Pitcher press扎壶操作
Key Ingredients关键原料
Ya-Shi lemon tea base, fragrant lemon, ice鸭屎香柠檬茶底、香水柠檬、冰块
  1. Press lemon and ice firmly, then pour into a 5L jug.将柠檬与冰块充分按压后倒入 5L 量杯。
  2. Add Ya-Shi lemon tea base and mix evenly.加入鸭屎香柠檬茶底并搅拌均匀。
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh lemon slices.满杯冰出品，并以新鲜柠檬片装饰。
02

Gardenia Tangerine Fruit Tea栀子油柑果茶

Category类别
Freshly pressed fruit tea鲜萃果茶
Method方式
Pitcher press扎壶操作
Key Ingredients关键原料
Gardenia tangerine base, fragrant lemon, ice栀子油柑茶底、香水柠檬、冰块
  1. Press fragrant lemon with ice to release aroma.将香水柠檬与冰块按压，释放香气。
  2. Add the tea base, mix, and remove lemon slices after blending.加入茶底搅拌，混合完成后取出柠檬片。
  3. Fill cup with ice, pour the prepared base, and garnish.杯中加满冰块，倒入茶底并完成装饰。
03

Rose Roselle Vinegar Fruit Tea玫瑰洛神醋果茶

Category类别
Freshly pressed fruit tea鲜萃果茶
Method方式
Pitcher press扎壶操作
Key Ingredients关键原料
Rose roselle vinegar base, yellow lemon, ice玫瑰洛神醋茶底、黄柠檬、冰块
  1. Press yellow lemon to prepare the fruit aroma base.按压黄柠檬，准备果香基底。
  2. Mix with rose roselle vinegar base in a 5L jug.在 5L 量杯中加入玫瑰洛神醋茶底并混合。
  3. Serve with ice and fresh lemon garnish.加冰出品，并以新鲜柠檬装饰。

04

Da Hong Pao Fresh Milk Tea大红袍鲜奶茶

Category类别
Fresh extract milk tea原萃液奶茶
Method方式
5L batch preparation5L 量壶预调
Key Ingredients关键原料
Da Hong Pao extract, milk, creamer, sugar大红袍萃取液、牛奶、植脂末、白砂糖
  1. Combine sugar, creamer, milk, extract, and purified water.混合白砂糖、植脂末、牛奶、萃取液与纯净水。
  2. Stir until fully dissolved and evenly blended.搅拌至充分溶解并均匀融合。
  3. Pour over ice to the service line.倒入加冰杯中至出品刻度线。

Ceylon Lemon Tea锡兰柠檬茶

Category类别
Fresh extract lemon tea原萃液柠檬茶

Key Ingredients关键原料
Ceylon black tea extract, sugar, yellow lemon锡兰红茶萃取液、白砂糖、黄柠檬

Key Ingredients关键原料
Kumquat juice, green tangerine juice, jasmine tea extract金桔汁、青柑汁、茉莉茶萃取液

Grape Oolong Lemon Tea葡萄乌龙柠檬茶

Category类别
Fresh extract fruit tea原萃液果茶
Method方式
Batch preparation with lemon press批量预调搭配柠檬按压

Grape jam, Ya-Shi Dancong tea, fragrant lemon葡萄果酱、鸭屎香单丛茶、香水柠檬

Peach Oolong Night蜜光乌龙夜

Category类别
Bar and mixer application酒吧与特调应用
Suitable For适用场景
Bar, music lounge, dining bar酒吧、音乐清吧、餐酒吧

Peach oolong extract, lemon juice, hop syrup, sparkling water蜜桃乌龙萃取液、柠檬汁、啤酒花糖浆、气泡水
