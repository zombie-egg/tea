Freshly Pressed Fruit Tea Series鲜萃果茶系列

① Ya-Shi Aromatic Lemon Tea鸭屎香柠檬茶

Category: Freshly Pressed Fruit Tea类别：鲜萃果茶
Suitable for: All F&B venues适用范围：所有餐饮门店
Preparation method: Pitcher press (扎壶操作)
Steps: 4 steps tea base prep + 3 steps serve步骤：4步茶底制作 + 3步出品
Key ingredients: Ya-Shi Aromatic Lemon Tea base · Fresh fragrant lemon · Ice关键原料：鸭屎香柠檬茶底 · 新鲜香水柠檬 · 冰块
Tea base preparation茶底制作：

Add 10 slices of fragrant lemon and 5 ice cubes to a cocktail shaker. Press firmly 20 times.将10片香水柠檬和5块冰块放入摇酒壶中。用力按压20次。

Pour pressed lemon into a 5L measuring jug.将按压后的柠檬倒入5L量杯中。

Add 2 bottles of Ya-Shi Aromatic Lemon Tea base. Mix well.加入2瓶鸭屎香柠檬茶底。搅拌均匀。

Remove lemon slices after mixing.搅拌完成后，取出柠檬片。

Serve出品

Fill serving cup with ice to the brim.至杯口）。

Pour prepared tea base to fill.倒入制作好的茶底至满杯。

Garnish with 8 slices of fresh fragrant lemon.用8片新鲜香水柠檬进行装饰。

④ Da Hong Pao Fresh Milk Tea大红袍鲜奶茶

Category: Fresh Extract — Milk Tea
Suitable for: All F&B venues
Preparation method: Batch prep (量壶预调)

Tea base preparation (5L batch):

Add to a 5L jug: 280g sugar · 200g coffee creamer · 700g Nestlé whole milk · 500g Da Hong Pao extract · 1500ml purified water. Mix until fully combined.
Serve:
Fill serving cup halfway with ice.
Pour prepared milk tea base to the ice level line.

⑥ Ceylon Lemon Tea锡兰柠檬茶

Category: Fresh Extract — Lemon Tea
Suitable for: All F&B venues
Preparation method: Batch prep

Tea base preparation (5L batch):

Add 650g white sugar and 500ml hot water to a 5L jug. Stir until sugar dissolves.
Add 1 bottle of Ceylon black tea extract and 1500ml purified water. Mix well.
Lemon tea assembly:
Take a 5L jug and add 15 slices of yellow lemon. Press 25 times with a muddler.
Pour the prepared Ceylon tea base over the pressed lemon.
Serve:
Fill serving vessel halfway with ice.

Pour lemon tea base to the ice level line.

⑧ Grape Oolong Lemon Tea葡萄乌龙柠檬茶

Category: Fresh Extract — Fruit Tea
Suitable for: All F&B venues
Preparation method: Batch prep
Tea base preparation:
Combine in a 5L jug: 500g sugar · 550g Kyoho grape jam · 900ml Ya-Shi Dancong tea · 2250ml purified water. Mix well.
In a cocktail shaker, add 10 slices of fragrant lemon. Press firmly 20 times.
Pour pressed lemon into the 5L jug and mix.

Serve:

Bar & Cocktail Mixer Series酒吧特调系列

⑨ Peach Oolong Night蜜光乌龙夜

Category: Bar & Mixer Application
Suitable for: Bar · Music lounge · Dining bar
Cost per serve: approx. 15 RMB / pitcher

Single cup (500ml):

Add to serving cup: 100g Peach Oolong extract · 50g yellow lemon juice · 25g hop sugar syrup. Mix well.
Fill cup with ice to the brim.
Top with sparkling water to fill.
Garnish with 3 mint leaves, lightly slapped.

Batch format (3L pitcher):

In a 5L jug combine: 2000ml Peach Oolong extract · 1000g yellow lemon juice · 500g hop sugar syrup. Mix well.
Serve:
Add 1050g peach mix to large serving vessel.
Fill vessel with ice to 80% full.
Top with 900ml sparkling water. Mix gently.
Garnish with 8 mint leaves, lightly slapped.
