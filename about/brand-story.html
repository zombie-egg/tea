From a Tea Factory in Chaozhou to Beverage Brands Around the World从潮州的一家茶厂，走向全球各地的饮品品牌

TeaSourcex is the international face of Yuanjin Group — a tea and beverage manufacturing group with roots in origin-region production and a mission to bring China's finest tea ingredients to the global market. TeaSourcexYuanjin Group）的国际品牌形象代表。作为一家深耕原产地生产的茶饮制造集团，元锦集团致力于将中国最优质的茶叶原料推向全球市场。

Where It Started起点

In 2007, Yuanjin Group was established in Guangzhou with a single conviction: that the quality of a beverage begins with the quality of its tea — and that quality starts at the source.2007年，远进集团在广州成立，秉持着一个信念：饮品的品质始于茶叶的品质——而茶叶的品质则始于源头。
China produces more tea varieties, in more distinct origin regions, than anywhere else in the world. Yet most of that depth and diversity never reaches the international beverage market. Buyers overseas were working with generic black tea concentrates and commodity extracts — not because better options did not exist, but because no one had built the supply chain to deliver them reliably, at scale, with the documentation that international trade requires.中国生产的茶叶品种比世界上任何其他国家都多，产地也更加独特。然而，如此丰富的茶叶资源和多样性却鲜少进入国际饮品市场。海外买家只能使用普通的红茶浓缩液和商品提取物——并非因为没有更好的选择，而是因为没有人建立起能够可靠、大规模地提供这些产品，并满足国际贸易所需单据的供应链。

That became the mission.这成为了远进集团的使命。

What Drives Us驱动我们的使命

Product Innovation产品创新

The global beverage market is moving toward authenticity — consumers want to know what is in their drink, where it came from, and why it tastes the way it does. We believe that Chinese tea, with its extraordinary range of varieties, origins, and processing traditions, has more to offer the international beverage market than it currently does.全球饮料市场正趋向于追求“本真”——消费者渴望确切了解饮品中含有何种成分、原料源自何处，以及为何呈现出独特的风味。我们坚信，中国茶凭借其极其丰富的品种、产地及加工传统，理应在国际饮料市场中发挥比目前更为举足轻重的作用。

Our role is to make that possible — through extraction technology that preserves what makes each tea distinctive, through formulation expertise that turns raw ingredients into products that work at commercial scale, and through a product development process that starts with the tea, not with the brief.我们的使命正是将这一愿景变为现实：通过先进的萃取技术，原汁原味地保留每一种茶的独特风韵；凭借专业的配方研发专长，将天然原料转化为可实现商业化规模生产的成品；并确立一套以“茶”为核心而非仅凭“需求简报”驱动的产品开发流程。

Export Mission出口使命

TeaSourcex exists because the gap between what Chinese tea manufacturing can offer and what international buyers can currently access is too large. Factory-direct supply at international quality standards, with the certifications and documentation that global trade requires, should not be difficult to find. We are building the supply chain that makes it straightforward.TeaSourcex 之所以应运而生，正是为了弥合一道巨大的鸿沟——即中国茶叶制造的实际供给能力，与国际买家目前所能获取的资源之间存在的巨大落差。按国际质量标准执行的工厂直供模式，辅以全球贸易所必需的各类认证与完备文档，本不应是难以寻觅的稀缺资源。如今，我们正致力于构建一条全新的供应链，让这一切变得触手可及、顺畅无阻。

Every client we work with — whether a first-time brand founder or an established beverage manufacturer — gets access to the same manufacturing capability, the same R&D team, and the same commitment to getting the product right. Origin tea, made to travel the world.无论客户是初涉行业的品牌创始人，还是久负盛名的资深饮料制造商，每一位与我们携手的伙伴都能享受到同等卓越的制造能力、同等专业的研发团队，以及我们对于打造完美产品的同样坚定承诺。源自原产地的纯正好茶，专为走向世界而生。
