Why Vertical Integration Matters垂直整合为什么重要

For buyers, a vertically integrated supplier means one thing above everything else: fewer points of failure.对于采购商而言，拥有垂直整合能力的供应商意味着最重要的一点：更少的潜在故障点。
When your tea ingredient passes through a trading company, a processing facility, and a packaging operation — each owned by a different entity — quality can drift at every handoff and traceability becomes difficult to maintain.当您的茶叶原料依次流经贸易公司、加工厂和包装车间——且每一环节均由不同的实体所有时——在每一次交接过程中，产品质量都可能发生偏差，且全程的可追溯性将难以维系。

Our supply chain runs from our own tea gardens and origin factories through to finished product in your specified format. Every step is controlled, documented, and auditable. When you need to know where a batch came from, we can tell you — within 24 hours, with documentation.我们的供应链体系涵盖从自营茶园、原产地初制工厂，直至按您指定规格交付成品的每一个环节。每一个步骤均处于严格管控之下，并留有详实记录，全程可供审计追溯。若您需要查证某一批次产品的具体来源，我们能在24小时内为您提供确切信息及相关证明文件。
